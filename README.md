AI Footprint: 

A Chrome Extension to track your carbon footprint real-time from your AI usage.


AI Footprint tracks your time spent on major AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini) and uses it to calculate estimated CO2 emisions based on this formula: 
Carbon Emissions (kg CO2) = Compute Hours x Power Draw (kW) x Power Usage Effectiveness x Grid Carbon Intensity (kg CO2/kWh)
The extension displays a live stopwatch and emissions value within a simple popup UI.
